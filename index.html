TAT figures reflect the imaging service level only (wait + service + hold time within that modality), not the full patient journey including billing and dispatch. "Pending" bundles Noshow, Pending, Standby, Serving, and E. Complete statuses (and their "-DONE" suffixed variants) i.e. every assigned token that isn't a full Complete. The hourly chart shows exact token counts and average TAT per hour, based on when each token was issued.
